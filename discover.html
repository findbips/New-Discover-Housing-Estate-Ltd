Discover Housing Estate Ltd.

Made possible by three gifted people determined to make the real estate of Bangladesh, a sector well known for transparency, professionalism, and reliability, our inception in 2017 was Discover Real Estate Ltd. Our journey started with the guarantee of being the most honest and outstanding in the field, and we were unchanging until now. Since this time we have been the ones to instill confidence in investors, sellers, and buyers, we are now a respected name in the industry.

We rebranded ourselves on January 1, 2025, as Discover Housing Estate Ltd. as the company was growing and responding to changes in the property market. This process represents our commitment to introducing cutting edge and practical real estate ideas. At present, we concentrate mostly on property acquisition, sales, and renting as well as on offering construction services that combine quality and affordability. Not to mention all across the nation, our portfolio covers a great variety of Dhaka choices, each with mobile property experiences that suit the demands of technology savvy homebuyers and investors are through this firm, Design Housing Estate Ltd., paving the path to long term partnerships, job generation, and community building.
